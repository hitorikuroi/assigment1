NOIR RECIPE BOOK.

This recipe compilation is meant to be a part of an interactive story, aiming to bring a unique experience to all diners that happen to come across it. We believe food is much more than eating, it entails a soul; a spirit, and in this particular case a genre passed from story teller to listener. A cop, a body guard, a mafia boss and an aspiring singer all bear in common ONE thing ... food! A range of emotions all represented by different dishes, carefully crafted to tell a story. Taking inspiration from Italian roots, and Sicilian mafia tales to Asian alleys and memories of gang encounters in street side restaurants. A melting pot of city buildings at night, cold cities, bullets and punches; and the multiple foods that accompanied it all!
